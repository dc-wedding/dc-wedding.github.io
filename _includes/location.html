We're getting married in the historic Hill Center in Washington, DC. It's on Pennsylvania Ave, down the road from the U.S. Capital. There's even a coffee shop on the grounds.


Where to stay.

We know a lot of people like to shop around or use rewards points for their hotel stays, so we do not have a designated hotel for the wedding. We recommend staying just across the Potomac in Virginia (which is where we live). The hotels there are cheaper than those in DC.

For choosing a hotel we recommend staying in one of two areas:

Arlington, VA

Arlington is where we live and is accessable by the Orange and Silver metro lines. There are hotels near all the main metro stations (Ballston, Virginia Square, Clarendon, Courthouse, and Rosslyn stations). At least one extra get-together will be held in Arlington, close to the Clarendon metro station.

Crystal City, VA

Pentagon City and Crystal City metro stations are off the Blue and Yellow lines. On the Blue and Yellow from Pentagon City/Crystal City you'll have a shorter trip to the district, but may need to change lines depending on where you want to go in DC.
